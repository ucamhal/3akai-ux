What's changing?

New HEFCE policy requires peer-reviewed articles and conference proceedings to be uploaded to an institutional repository when they are accepted for publication, to be eligible for the next REF.

The implication is that the University needs to capture 100% of Accepted Author Manuscripts at the time of acceptance, to be ready for the next REF.

This service is the beginning of our response to that challenge.
